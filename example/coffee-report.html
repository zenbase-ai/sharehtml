Global Coffee Production & Consumption Trends

Jan 2024 – Dec 2025 | generated 2025-12-18
Summary
  • Global coffee production reached 11.4M metric tons in 2025, up 14% from 10.0M in 2024, driven primarily by a record Brazilian harvest.
  • Arabica's share of total output rose from 57% to 62%, reversing a three-year trend toward Robusta dominance.
  • Per-capita consumption in East Asia grew 31% YoY, making it the fastest-growing demand region for the second year running.
  • Average green bean spot price fell 8% ($4.12/kg to $3.79/kg) as supply growth outpaced demand.
